moscow visitor

If you do not know what to visit before your trip to Moscow, then we
are happy to help you. We have prepared for you the most
interesting places that will definitely interest you, so that you can
get to know the Russian culture and architecture better.

TSARITSYNO PARK

Monument of Russian Gothic architecture, built in the 18th century by order of Catherine II.

VDNHK

The former exhibition center of the entire USSR, now one of the most beautiful places in Moscow for walking and recreation.

MOSCOW-CITY

Moscow International Business Center, with an observation deck, shopping center and beautiful views.
